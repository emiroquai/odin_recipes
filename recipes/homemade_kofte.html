Homemade Kofte

Description

This timeless classic is a Turkish homecooking staple. It will make all Turks revisit their childhood memories of their moms' cooking

The recipe is fairly simple so no reason to get intimidated, let's dig in!

Ingredients

  • 500gr minced beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cup Parsley/li>
  • 1 tspCumin
  • 1 tbsp Corn flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and chop the parsley.
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl and let it rest in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  3. Knead the mixture into medium sized balls.
  4. Fry in a pan for 2 minutes per side.
